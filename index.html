Welcome to YOUWIN Connect!

Our mission is to ignite Nigeria’s economic growth by empowering young entrepreneurs and supporting Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) through enterprise education, capacity building, and strategic support. Join us on the journey to create jobs, stimulate industries, and build a resilient economy

About YOUWIN

Get to know about YOUWIN Connect.

General Background:

The YOUWIN (Youth Enterprise with Innovation in Nigeria) project was launched by the Federal Ministry of Finance between 2011 and 2015 as a youth-focused business plan competition. Through this initiative, young Nigerians were awarded grants to start or expand businesses, promoting economic growth and job creation nationwide. YOUWIN has since evolved into YOUWIN Connect, focusing on enterprise education to support emerging and established businesses across Nigeria’s key sectors.

Our Mission:

YOUWIN Connect promotes entrepreneurship as a career path for young Nigerians, encouraging innovation and resilience in critical sectors like Agriculture, ICT, Manufacturing, and more. We aim to bridge the skills gap, inspire budding entrepreneurs, and contribute to Nigeria’s economic development through targeted training and mentorship.

Our Vision:

To drive Nigeria's economic recovery and growth through SME-led development, creating jobs, fostering innovation, and nurturing a culture of entrepreneurship.

Enterprise Education

TYOUWIN Connect’s core mission is to educate and inspire the next generation of entrepreneurs through a multimedia approach. Our programs are accessible through print, TV, radio, and digital media, delivering valuable insights and training to help participants PLAN, START, and GROW their businesses

Capacity Building

  • Online Training: Year-round online training to enhance skills in Marketing, Financial and HR Management, and Business Operations.
  • Masterclass Training: Intensive in-person training for top performers in sectors like Fashion, Manufacturing, and Agriculture, conducted in collaboration with partners such as Lagos Business School, ACCA, and Google.

Franchise Infrastructure Development

This initiative supports young entrepreneurs by promoting franchise models across Nigeria, fostering job creation, and enhancing income potential in key sectors like Agriculture, ICT, and Manufacturing. Franchise hubs have been established in each geopolitical zone to boost regional economic growth.

Enterprise Education on Campus/NYSC Camps:

Through events in tertiary institutions and NYSC camps, YOUWIN Connect reaches aspiring young entrepreneurs, exposing them to skills and opportunities for starting and scaling their businesses.

The National Youth Unification and Business Support (NYUBS) is a platform for empowering youth through entrepreneurship and skill development.

Youth participants connect with business mentors, gain access to training programs, and explore grant opportunities to support their professional growth and business ventures.
