The Honourable Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ambassador Yusuf M. Tuggar (OON) received in audience a team of 18 Scholars from the Schwarzman Scholars Programme at Tsinghua University in Beijing, China, led by Ms. Tami Austen-Peters today 2nd May, 2024.
The Schwarzman Scholars Programme affords selected scholars from different countries the opportunity to develop their leadership skills through a fully-funded one-year Master’s degree in Global Affairs. It is also designed to build a global community of future Leaders who will strengthen understanding between their countries and China as well as with the rest of the world.
The Honourable Minister welcomed the Team and informed them about the 4-Ds Foreign Policy thrust (Democracy, Development, Demography, and Diaspora) of the Nigerian Government. He further enlightened them about Nigeria’s history, culture and development drive.

Nasir Isa Kwarra https://foreignaffairs.gov.ng/ambassador-yusuf-m-tuggar-oon-received-in-audience-hon-nasir-isa-kwarra/ https://foreignaffairs.gov.ng/ambassador-yusuf-m-tuggar-oon-received-in-audience-hon-nasir-isa-kwarra/#respond Wed, 17 Apr 2024 14:25:28 +0000 https://foreignaffairs.gov.ng/?p=251545 The Honourable Minister of Foreign Affairs, Ambassador Yusuf M. Tuggar (OON) received in audience Hon. Nasir Isa Kwarra, Chairman of the National Population Commission (NPC) who led a delegation of UNFPA on a visit to the Ministry today, 16th April, 2024. On the delegation was Mr. Ojogun Tellson Osifo, Director General, NPC, and Mr. Koessan Francis Kuawu, Deputy Resident Representative, UNFPA among others.
Discussions centred on the Commission’s preparedness to organize credible census in Nigeria. In line with the 4-Ds Foreign Policy thrust of President Bola Ahmed Tinubu’s Government, the Honourable Minister underscored the importance of Nigeria’s demographic dividend and welcomed efforts towards enhancing synergy between the Ministry and NPC.
The meeting also discussed the upcoming official visit to Nigeria by Dr. Natalia Kanem, UNFPA’s Executive Director.
